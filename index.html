    • INTRODUCTION:

    Sindh Desert Development Organization with its acronym SDDO is a nongovernmental, nonprofitable development organization, established in year 2005 and registered under the social welfare Act 1961 Registration# F.DO-SW-CD (U.KOT) R-VSWA/2009/16. SDDO was established by the group of young dedicated people, concerned with the prevailing socio economic & cultural backwardness of the underprivileged communities. Witnessing no sigh of relief from any other quarters, they embarked upon the mission to bring the marginalized communities to the mainstream of life through capacity building and participatory efforts. The community gradually shaped in form of SDDO to claim their social political, economic and human rights in highly commendable way.

    VISION

    SDDO is committed to bring positive changes in the society by promoting gender equality, literacy ratio and self-reliance. It works to bring peace and harmony to economically stable poverty free educated society.

    MISSION

    We strive for the sustainable development of communities focusing preservation, promotion and trongly believe that the best way to poverty reduction, is not only through imparting quality Education but generating skillful youth and emphasizing self-empowerment. Our strategy for implementation rather focuses on:

  • Revitalizing Youth Enterprise
  • Community mobilization and adoptability of local environment
  • Gender equality
  • Women empowerment through vocational training
  • Career counselling and job placement through SDDO help cell
  • Facilitation and support through collaborations and social interaction with employment businesses and market

    • Values

    • OBJECTIVES

  • To promote primary education.
